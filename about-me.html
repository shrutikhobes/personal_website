My Research Work

Research Projects done at NLPRL, IIT-BHU under the guidance of Dr. A. K. Singh (Associate Professor at the Department of Computer Science, IIT-BHU)

Keywords: Natural language processing, deep learning, sequence-2-sequence network, Attention mechanism, Part-of-speech tagging

  • The Neural Machine Translation using Attention Mechanism- Independent study on NMT with attention. Executed it by making a model to represent the process. Model used the sequence to sequence (seq2seq) network for translating thousands of [lang1] words (sentences) to [lang2] by developing an attention plot which is an easy-to-visualize alignment matrix between the source and target sentences.

  • Word Embedding for Neural Networks- Developed a series of models of word embeddings in Neural Networks for the Indian languages using fastText and Word2Vec. Explored research and careers by comprehensive paper summaries and tangential readings.

Research Projects done under the guidance of Dr. Brijendra Gupta (PhD, IIT-BHU). (Ongoing)

Keywords: Bioinspired Machine Learning, Bioinformatics, Genomics Metaheuristics, ANN, RNN, CNN

  • EEG Brain Simulation- Electroencephalography (EEG) Brain Simulation for Amyotrophic lateral sclerosis (ALS) patients. Using the techniques of ANN, RNN & CNN as image analysis tools for the precision of medicine.

  • Coronavirus Optimization Algorithms- A bioinspired metaheuristic based on the Covid-19 propagation model. Comparative evaluation on the different methods used, and the idea of approach towards Genetic algorithm in drug design to suppress Covid fitness.
